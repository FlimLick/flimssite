All about me

Hi, I'm Ashton Clouse. I've been programming since I was 9, mostly in C++, chasing the moment when hardware does the impossible.

I started with C++ and later picked up Python, then moved into Unity and eventually found my rhythm in Godot with GDScript.

C++ Godot Audio Networking

Background

My main focus is building game systems, experimenting with multiplayer, and creating audio pipelines that feel alive. I keep a long list of languages in rotation: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Java, F#, SQL, Python, C++, ASM x86 (32-64 bit), GD Script, Ruby, Swift, PHP, and Rust.

Outside of coding I love music and audio engineering. I'm in a band called Sounds Of Absurdity, and I handle soundtracks, sound design, and mixing for my projects.

To learn systems deeply and turn experiments into playable worlds.
